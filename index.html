Knoxville Highlights

🏛️
Market Square
Downtown · 0.3 mi from hotel
The social heart of Knoxville — a historic square lined with restaurants, bars, and a beloved farmers market. Lively day and night, and steps from Oliver Royale.
Neighborhood
🎶
Old City
Arts District · 0.5 mi from hotel
Knoxville's creative quarter — indie bars, live music venues, street art, and eclectic restaurants tucked into repurposed historic buildings. The city's coolest pocket.
Neighborhood

Downtown · On the itinerary
James Beard-nominated Chef Joseph Lenn's flagship. Wood-fired cooking, local farms, and an intimate dining room that feels earned. Consistently ranked among Tennessee's best.
